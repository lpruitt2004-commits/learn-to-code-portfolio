# FAQ & Troubleshooting: Career & Agile

## Common Questions

**Q: How do I stand out to employers?**
- Showcase projects and skills on your resume and LinkedIn.
- Network and ask for referrals.

**Q: Struggling with interviews?**
- Practice with mock interviews and review feedback.

**Q: Team conflict?**
- Communicate openly and focus on solutions.
