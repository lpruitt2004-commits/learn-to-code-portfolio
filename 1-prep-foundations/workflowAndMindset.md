# Workflow and Mindset

Success in coding comes from good habits, effective workflows, and a growth mindset.

## Study Habits
- Set regular study times
- Take notes and summarize what you learn
- Practice by building small projects

## Project Management
- Break tasks into small steps
- Use checklists or kanban boards
- Track your progress

## Communication
- Ask questions when stuck
- Share your work for feedback
- Collaborate with peers

## Growth Mindset
- Embrace challenges
- Learn from mistakes
- Celebrate progress
